## Stage 1 — Position Statement

### Title of Proposed Change

> PROC-001/PROC-002 amendment package: dual-anchor pre-flight verification to the Evaluator R1 checklist (CAND-A), and enforce the Coverage Justification heading + codify the static-artifact carve-out permission test (CAND-B).

### Proposed Change

> **CAND-A — amend PROC-001.** Today PROC-001 prescribes a *Generator-side* pre-flight verification step recorded in a Technical Notes `Pre-flight verification` subsection. Across all three Cycle 3 contracts that subsection was never surfaced, yet the defect-prevention outcome PROC-001 targets *was* achieved — by the Evaluator's pre-implementation baseline verification at R1. The amendment **dual-anchors** the obligation: (1) retain the Generator-side step as aspirational, and (2) add a mandatory **Evaluator R1 checklist item** — the Evaluator affirmatively records in the R1 review that it executed each non-null `verification_command` against pre-sprint state, and flags any contract lacking a `Pre-flight verification` subsection as an advisory. This converts the de-facto Evaluator practice into ratified standard work.
>
> **CAND-B — amend PROC-002.** Two additive clauses: (1) the `Coverage Justification` heading becomes **mandatory verbatim** when behavioral coverage ∈ [60%, 65%] (S11 grazed the band at 62% but used a non-conformant heading); (2) codify the **static-artifact carve-out permission test** — the carve-out is permitted only when (a) all deliverables are non-runnable static artifacts with no shell entry point, (b) the Technical Notes state coverage both with and without any "simulation behavioral" criterion, and (c) the precedent sprint is cited. This is the exact ladder S12 used correctly (and S11 used loosely).
>
> Files affected: `.council/standard-work.json` (PROC-001 and PROC-002 `steps`/`description` fields amended additively; version bump 1.1.0 → 1.2.0). Scope: methodology procedures consumed by Generator (contract drafting) and Evaluator (R1 review); no plugin source or harness runtime change.

### Trigger / Motivation

> Surfaced by the **retrospective agent** (pdca mode) in the Cycle 3 PDCA Check #1 and Check #2, and corroborated by the **architect** (L2 review, findings F1–F8 with F7 confirming routing). If not made: PROC-001 remains a dead-letter Generator procedure that does not describe the active defect-prevention mechanism (Evaluator pre-baselining), and PROC-002's carve-out has no permission boundary — leaving the S12-style sub-floor carve-out un-gated and the Coverage Justification heading optional (so S11-style non-conformance recurs silently). Linked Henkaten: HK-0009 (behavioral-coverage trend, responded) and its S12 update recommendation (i).
